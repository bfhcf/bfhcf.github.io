About Us

Vision

We are a community of Worshippers of the Triune God, Image-bearers in identity and impact.

Mission

To Glorify God by Discipling God's people to become loving and compassionate Disciple-makers, and as the Father gathers His elect, nurture them to Christ-likeness and, through the Word of God and the leading of the Holy Spirit, Transform communities in the Philippines.

Get Involved

CALL

Refers to the Call of the Father for His elect. It deals with the gathering of His elect as provided in our Church Mission through evangelism.

CULTIVATE

Refers to the growth of a believer in the faith to become Spiritually mature. It aims to help a believer grow from infancy to adulthood in his Christian living. It also refers to intentional discipleship as each believer is nurtured to Christlikeness. Nurturing of the spirit.

CARE

Refers to the outpouring of the gifts of the Holy Spirit in nurturing the physical aspect of a believer.
