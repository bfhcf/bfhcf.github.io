5-Minute Journey

Through the Storms in His Presence

Rev. Victorio G. San Juan

March 15, 2020, when our Government declared the first-ever lockdown in Metro Manila and placed the area of over 12 million people in a state of community quarantine. Two days after, a stricter measure, the Enhanced Community Quarantine (ECQ), was implemented over the island of Luzon, including its associated islands.

Before that, as the assigned pastor, I made a plan on what to do. Then the lockdown came! I was baffled and did not know what to do. No matter how good my schedule was, I could not implement it. Being a doer person, I was at a loss.

So, I find myself praying and asking GOD what He wants me to do. That's when the 5-minute came to my mind. Its only purpose is to walk a 5-minute Journey with the LORD during this pandemic. As we journey with the LORD, the 5-minute Journey looks at the events and issues related to COVID-19. With the Bible as the guide and source of strength and wisdom, it could minister throughout one year.

Book Contents

This devotional book offers spiritual guidance to help you navigate unexpected challenges encountered during our lives.

365 Reflections

Offering daily guidance on coping with the stresses and challenges of a pandemic, providing solace and perspective in uncertain times.

Old Testament Insights

Delving into the lives of Old Testament characters, exploring how they maintained their relationship with God amidst adversities.

New Testament Wisdom

Reflecting on the vital teachings of our Lord Jesus and the epistles of the New Testament, drawing essential truths for modern living.
