Courage In Chaos

Devotional Encouragement for Anxious Hearts

Dino D. Poblete

In the face of our struggles, we find comfort in knowing that we are not alone. God's presence serves as an anchor, providing us with strength to lean on. Through prayer, meditation, and the exploration of scripture, we unlock profound truths that can illuminate even our darkest moments, guiding us toward a path of light.

I have grappled with health anxiety and stress throughout my journey of seeking relief for my chronic pains. However, amidst this journey, I have come to realize that the true remedy lies in Christ. Engaging in daily meditations and communing with God has not only given peace but also inspired me to document my reflections and compile them into meaningful mini devotionals.

This collection serves as a companion for those navigating their own anxieties, seeking peace and inspiration to navigate life's complexities. It stands as a testament to the power of faith, the resilience of the human spirit, and the transformative potential that lies in embracing God's presence.

About the Author

Dino Antonio D. Poblete

Dino is a deacon and a dedicated member of Bread From Heaven Christian Fellowship, where he has faithfully served in various capacities for over 15 years. Initially starting in the music ministry as an instrumentalist, he later became a worship leader. Additionally, Dino serves as a speaker for the Young People's Fellowship and leads the multimedia ministry within the church. While his professional career lies in software engineering, he prioritizes his ministry alongside his primary role as a devoted husband and father to his wife and two boys.
